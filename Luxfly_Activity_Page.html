FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is indoor skydiving?
Indoor skydiving recreates the sensation of freefall using a powerful vertical wind tunnel. You float on a column of air at speeds up to 300 km/h — the exact same feeling as jumping from a plane at 4,000 meters, but in a completely safe, controlled environment.
Is it safe? Do I need experience?
Absolutely! Indoor skydiving is 100% safe with no risk of vertigo. No prior experience is needed — a qualified instructor is with you the entire time, guiding every second of your flight. Ages 4 and up are welcome.
How long does the experience last?
The total experience is approximately 2 hours, including check-in, safety briefing, gearing up, your flights, and time to enjoy the museum and bar area afterward.
What should I wear?
Wear comfortable clothing and lace-up shoes (no loose items). We provide the full flight suit, helmet, goggles, and ear protection. Long hair should be tied back.
Are there any restrictions?
Maximum weight is 120 kg for safety reasons. People who have had a shoulder dislocation cannot participate due to associated risks. Pregnant women should consult their doctor first.
Where is Luxfly located?
Luxfly is at Rue De Grass 103, 6700 Sterpenich — right on the Luxembourg/Belgium border. Easy access from Luxembourg City (25 min), Brussels (2h), and the greater region.
