FAQ

Birthday Party FAQ

What ages can participate?
Birthday packages are designed for children aged 4–15. Younger children can watch from the viewing gallery while older siblings and parents fly too!
Can we bring our own food?
You must bring your own birthday cake. Other outside food is not permitted by default, but special arrangements can be made by emailing sales@luxfly.eu in advance. The on-site bar has drinks and snacks available.
What if a child can't make it?
No problem! Packages are non-nominative, meaning you can substitute children. Just let us know of any changes before the event.
Can parents fly too?
Absolutely! Parents can book individual flights or a family package alongside the birthday party. It's a fantastic way to join in the fun!
How far in advance should we book?
We recommend booking 2–4 weeks in advance, especially for weekend-adjacent time slots and school holiday periods. Contact us as early as possible for the best availability.
