FAQ

Corporate Event FAQ

How many people can you accommodate?
We accommodate groups from 2 to 250 people. Each event is customized to your group size, with rotating flight schedules so everyone gets their turn while others enjoy the catering and socializing areas.
Do you have meeting/presentation rooms?
Yes! Our facility includes dedicated meeting and presentation rooms with audiovisual equipment. Perfect for combining a strategy session or workshop with the flying activity.
What catering options are available?
We offer fully customizable catering — from coffee breaks and finger food to full sit-down meals and champagne receptions. Our team adapts to your budget and dietary requirements.
Can we book outside normal hours?
Absolutely. Luxfly is available 24/7 for reserved corporate events. Early morning, evening, and weekend bookings are all possible for private groups.
Do participants need experience?
None at all! Indoor skydiving is 100% safe and suitable for all fitness levels. Qualified instructors guide everyone through the experience. Ages 4+ and the maximum weight is 120 kg.
